About XLRI

Founded in 1949, XLRI is the oldest B-School and has consistently ranked among the top 3 MBA colleges in India. After producing generations of industry stalwarts, we are striding towards becoming an attractive destination for organisations to acquire their future Product Managers and CIOs. This year, approximately 33% of the batch of 470 students acquired Internship Offers from top tech firms for their coveted Product or Program Manager Roles. We have a strong batch size of 1000+ students and a reach across other MBA and Engineering Colleges across the country. Our social media pages have an active engagement of 100K+ followers and a footprint of 10K+ views per post.
